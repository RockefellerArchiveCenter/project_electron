About Project Electron


Project Electron is a major project to build sustainable, user-centered, and standards-compliant infrastructure to support the ongoing acquisition, management, preservation of digital records so that we can make them available in the broadest and most equitable way possible.

The project will consist of a repository, which will store digital records as well as information about them (including archival description, technical metadata and preservation metadata) which will help us manage and provide access to them over time.
